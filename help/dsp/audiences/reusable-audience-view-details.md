# Visa information om en återanvändbar publik

1. Klicka på **[!UICONTROL Audiences]** > **[!UICONTROL All audiences]** på huvudmenyn.

1. Hold the cursor over the audience row and click **[!UICONTROL View Details]**.

1. (Optional) To see the number of active audience members by device type (for cookie-based IDs) and by universal ID type, click ![Device breakdown](/help/dsp/assets/device-breakdown.png) next to the audience size.

1. (Optional) To edit the audience settings, click **[!UICONTROL Edit Audience]** and [edit the audience name and configuration](reusable-audience-edit.md).
